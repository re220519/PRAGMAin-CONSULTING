#1 Do you have a business plan?

Planning without action is Futile and action without plan is Fatal. You need to have a business plan in place before you start a business

#2 Do you have a passion for what you are planning to do?

Passion is energy. That drives you to success. So if you are not passionate about your business plan , don’t do that's it.

#3 Is it a unique idea? If not how is it different from others?

It is not necessary that every business is unique. What matters is how differently you do it from others. What makes your business different? Think about it before you start and speak about it after you start!

#4 Do you have self-discipline?

This is one of the most important qualities for an aspiring entrepreneur. Unlike a job, self-accountability drives you in your own business.

#5 Do you have a financial plan for the business as well as your family?

Lesser the distraction, better for your business. Plan your finance first.
